FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
FAQs – SMART4HOME
1.
What is Smart4Home?
It is fixed wireless access offer with discounted bundles.
2.
What is the difference from SmartBiz?
Smart4Home bundles are bundles specifically for the home user offering unrestricted speeds, whilst SmartBiz offers bundles with different
speed caps.
3.
Can I purchase the Smart4Home line only as I already have my own router?
No, the product is a combo, whereby on purchase the customer gets the router and Sim Card.
4.
Where can we find these bundles?
For the initial purchase of a router and Sim card the customer must visit an Econet shop. To purchase the Smart4Home bundles the
customer can visit *143# USSD, choose Option 1, Choose Option 8and then select the preferred bundle, enter the Smart4Home number
and choose payment option to complete the transaction.
5.
What is the validity of these bundles.
The bundles are all valid for 30days.
6.
Is this a promotion, and if so, how long will it last?
These are long term products that will be available for our customers going forward.

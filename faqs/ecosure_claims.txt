How to stop EcoSure from deducting money?

Here are the steps to follow if you want to disable the auto debit
facility and prevent an unwarranted $1 deduction from your EcoCash
wallet.
1. Dial *900#
2. Proceed to enter your EcoCash PIN.
3. On the menu select option 4: Manage Policy.
4. Select Option 2: Disable Premium Auto Debit.
5. Select Option 1 to Confirm.
How to sponsor family or friends that are registered on EcoSure?
1. Dial *900# , enter EcoCash PIN, select Funeral, Individual, USD/ZW.
2. Select Sponsor New Family Member & Friends.
3. Select Sponsor New Member and enter number to be sponsored.
4. Enter last 3 letters of the sponsored 's name.
5. Confirm.
